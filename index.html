Background on the Trial

On the night of January 28, 2022, Karen Read, & John O’Keefe went to the Waterfall Bar in Canton. According to the court, Karen Read had been drinking. Karen Read was meant to drop off John O’Keefe at an after-party. At around 6:00 am on January 29, 2022, John had still not made it back home. When first responders arrived, they claimed they heard Read saying, "I hit him. I hit him." (The defense argues this claim is false). An autopsy later revealed that the main cause of John O’Keefe’s death was due to blunt force trauma to the head, and hypothermia. Now, the trial rages on. Is Karen Read innocent or a murderer?
